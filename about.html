Welcome To “New Golden Aquarium”

New Golden Aquarium is a Pet Shop started in 2007 at Kalladikkode. Palakkad (Dist) Kerala .And still continuing its journey with a big number of satisfied and happy customers and beneficiaries. Later in Early 2013 we also started a branch in Tachampara which is 10 kms away from our 1st Branch. We are now the best and genuine dealers of various aquarium, aviary and kennel accessories in our locality..

We value our customers and their customised thoughts which adds up innovation and variety in our conventional organisational thoughts and ideas
